Hiking

Did you know that Hiking is nature's therapy? Many of us find it hard to quiete the mind and appreciate life through nature's beautiful creations. Hiking has become more popular over the years and is one of the most affordable recreational activities to participate in. It is a wonderful tool to gain silence and clarity in a world of constant chaos and stimulation. You are able to sweat out the tension and at the same time get the blood flowing in your veins.There are guided day hike tours which happen on a regular basis and we choose hikes based on your capability and eagerness for a challenge. What's great is that nomatter what shape you are in you are still able to reap the benefits from the overall hiking experience.

Camping

Camping is ideal for family vacations and socialising.What better time to bond with your family around the campfire scoffing down smoors. The campgrounds provide a bunch of interactive activities such as swimming and other sports for the whole family to enjoy. Did you know camping out in nature amongst the trees increases your serotonin levels which helps lift your mood. Talk about a happy camper!

Fishing

Do you get excited by reeling in a 20 pound Galjoen? Are you interested in becoming and Angler? If you answered yes, you have come to the right place. Fishing is a fun and therepeutic activity and helps you to form bonds with like-minded individuals who share the same interests around fishing. The thrill of fishing has a way of fulfilling an age-old need of pursuing and catching. By taking up this hobby you will become tremendously aware of the importance clean water and air and the conservation around the fishing enviroment.It is both a challenging and rewarding experience.

Rock Climbing

Rock Climbing puts your strength and endurance to the test. This sport not only strengthens your body but has that effect on your mind too through hand-and-eye co ordination. Rock Climbing relies heavily on a list of physical factors such as upper and lower body strength, intricate footwork and muscle mass. If you are looking to burn calories fast this is the ultimate sport for you. It will not only benefit your flexibility but your problem solving skills too"
